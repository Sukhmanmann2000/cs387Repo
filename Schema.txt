Each Node Explanation with its attributes

Movies 
    movie_id (primary key)
    title   (not null)
    url 
    year_released
    duration
    avg_rating
    no_user_ratings      (weight is 1)  
    no_critics_ratings   (weight is 5)  (Default 0)

Genres
    name (primary key)

is_genre
    from Movies and Genres

Celebrity
    id   (primary key)
    name (not null)
    gender
    dob

acted_in
    from celebrity to Movies (for actors)

directed
    from celebrity to Movies (for directors)
